# Privacy Policy for PracticeTime!

**PracticeTime!** is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our mobile application.

## 1. No Collection of Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
PracticeTime! does **not** collect, store, or process any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). This includes, but is not limited to:
* Names
* Email addresses (except as noted in section 3)
* Phone numbers
* Physical addresses
* Device identifiers that could be linked to an individual

## 2. No Data Transmission
The application is designed to function entirely offline.
* **Local Storage:** All data created within the app, such as practice sessions, goals, and categories, is stored locally on your device's internal storage.
* **No External Servers:** PracticeTime! does not send your data to any external servers, cloud services, or third-party analytics platforms.
* **No Tracking:** We do not use cookies, web beacons, or any other tracking technologies to monitor your activity.

## 3. Permissions
The application may request certain permissions (such as Microphone for the Tuner/Recorder or Notifications for running sessions). These permissions are used exclusively for the functionality of the app on your device. Audio recorded via the Recorder feature remains strictly on your device's local storage.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version of the application and this document.
